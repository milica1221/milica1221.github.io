Smart Building

By leveraging Internet of Things (IoT) devices, artificial intelligence (AI), and data analytics, smart building solutions offer a range of benefits for property owners and managers, including energy efficiency, cost savings, and improved occupant comfort.

Smart Building Solutions for Sustainable and Efficient Operations

At NovaVector, we're committed to helping businesses unlock the full potential of smart buildings. Our platform offers a range of data parameters that can help you optimize your building's performance, including Average Daily Occupancy, TVOC, CO2, Real-Time Occupancy, and Average Energy Consumption. With our user-friendly interface, you'll be able to quickly and easily access all the data you need to make informed decisions about your building's operations.

Real-Time Data Analytics

One of the key advantages of our smart building platform is the ability to access real-time data analytics. With our platform, you'll be able to see exactly how your building is performing at any given moment. From identifying areas of high energy consumption to tracking occupancy levels, our data analytics can help you make informed decisions that will save you time, money, and energy.

Interactive Building Maps

In addition to our data analytics, our platform also features interactive building maps. With our maps, you'll be able to quickly and easily locate any office within your building, as well as track occupancy levels and other key metrics. This can be incredibly useful for businesses with multiple offices, as it allows you to get a clear picture of how your building is being utilized.
