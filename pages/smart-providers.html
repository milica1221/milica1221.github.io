Service Providers

Nova Vector Platform offers a range of solutions that enable service providers to capitalize on the growing Internet of Things market, delivering new products and revenue streams in record time. Our platform can be used as an IaaS, PaaS, or SaaS solution, depending on your business needs.

Create New Products and Revenue Streams in Record Time

Service Providers face a new reality where providing connectivity and computing power is highly competitive domain. The profit margins are decreasing, user churn is increasing. The Internet of Things domain is growing and is predicted to grow in the foreseeable future. Adding IoT service is the logical next step for the connectivity and cloud providers because it can increase the utilization of their existing infrastructure while providing new products and revenue streams. It also helps keep existing customers because it can offer new services and help differentiate from the competition.

Nova Vector Platform as
IaaS Solution

Our IaaS solution enables service providers to offer their customers a flexible and scalable cloud computing model, without the need to manage servers, hardware, networking equipment, and data storage. With Nova Vector Platform, you can automate deployment, purchase hardware based on consumption, and retain complete control of your infrastructure.

Nova Vector Platform as
PaaS Solution

Our PaaS solution offers developers the freedom to concentrate on building applications, without worrying about operating systems, software updates, storage, or infrastructure. With Nova Vector Platform, you can easily develop and deploy apps, reduce coding time, automate business policy, and migrate to the hybrid model.

Nova Vector Platform as
SaaS Solution

Our SaaS solution eliminates the need for IT staff to download and install applications on individual computers. With Nova Vector Platform, you can deliver applications via the internet, streamline maintenance and support, and free up time for technical staff to focus on pressing issues within the organization.
