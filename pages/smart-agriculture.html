Smart Agriculture

Efficient agriculture depends on real-time monitoring of soil conditions. Nova Vector's platform offers the tools needed to gather precise data, optimize irrigation, and make better-informed decisions.

Get Knowledge of Soil Parameters

Nova Vector provides real-time soil parameter data through our platform and soil sensors. Access temperature, humidity, pH, and NPK levels on a user-friendly dashboard and streamline your agricultural practices.

Optimize Fertilizer Usage while Maximizing the Growth

Leverage real-time soil parameter insights with Nova Vector to optimize fertilizer application, promoting growth while conserving valuable resources.

Connect to Weather Services and Optimize Irrigation

The Nova Vector platform integrates with weather services and combines real-time weather data with current soil conditions, enhancing irrigation efficiency for improved agricultural output.
